Welcome Message and Description:

Upon running the script, the user is greeted with a welcome message and a description of the game's rules and objectives.
Gameplay:

The game involves guessing a word by selecting letters from the English alphabet.
The player has a boat with 5 lives. If the boat loses all its lives, it will drown.
Each time the player makes an incorrect guess, they lose a life.
When the player makes a correct guess, they are shown the updated word with the guessed letters revealed.
The game continues until the player guesses the entire word correctly or runs out of lives.
User Interaction:

The player interacts with the game by entering letters as guesses.
The player has the option to quit the game at any time by typing 'exit'.
Feedback:

The game provides feedback to the player after each guess, informing them whether their guess was correct or incorrect and how many lives they have remaining.
If the player has only one life remaining, a hint is provided to help them guess the word.
Win/Loss Conditions:

If the player correctly guesses the entire word, they win the game.
If the player runs out of lives before guessing the entire word, they lose the game.
Restart Option:

After the game ends (either by winning or losing), the player is prompted whether they want to play again. If they choose to play again, the game restarts.
